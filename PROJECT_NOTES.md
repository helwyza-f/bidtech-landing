# BidTech Landing Blueprint Notes

## Visual Direction

- Primary palette: black / charcoal base with neon green accent.
- Tone: premium software house, enterprise-friendly, modern, assertive.
- Key motif: hexagonal "B" mark, glowing edges, digital grid, futuristic overlays.
- Supporting imagery: corporate building render for trust and scale, tech-circle visual for innovation and digital execution.

## What The Blueprint Already Solves

- Brand recall is strong because the icon, color, and type treatment are distinctive.
- The visual system looks more "established company" than "individual freelancer".
- The green highlight is a usable CTA and section-accent color for the whole site.

## Messaging Direction For Meeting

- Core positioning: software house focused on business outcomes, not only coding.
- Suggested pitch:
  "BidTech helps businesses design, build, and scale digital solutions across web, mobile, and cloud."
- Target client perception:
  trustworthy, technically capable, responsive, modern, and ready for serious projects.

## Recommended Sections For This First Version

1. Hero with value proposition and CTA.
2. Service grid.
3. Short company profile / positioning.
4. Delivery process.
5. Contact CTA.

## Recommended Next Iteration After Meeting

- Add real portfolio projects or case studies.
- Add testimonials or client logos.
- Add founder/team section.
- Add contact form with WhatsApp CTA.
- Add domain email, company address, and social proof.
